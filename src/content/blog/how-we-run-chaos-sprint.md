Chaos Sprint looks intense, but it follows a clear rhythm.

## Format

1. Prompt reveal in the first 5 minutes
2. Team planning for 10 minutes
3. Rapid build phase for 60 minutes
4. Demo and review for final 15 minutes

## Why it works

The timebox forces decision making.
Teams learn to prioritize function over perfection.
